Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Taylan Ozgun ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the Geology Daily mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Geology Daily does not require account registration. The App collects the following data:

  • Anonymous user ID: A randomly generated identifier created via Firebase Anonymous Authentication. This is not linked to your name, email, or any personal identity.
  • Quiz scores and rankings: Your monthly quiz scores are stored on Firebase Firestore to power the global leaderboard.
  • Display name: The name you enter in the app settings (e.g. "Taylan") is stored alongside your score on the leaderboard. You may use any name you choose.
  • Local app data: Quiz progress, streak, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and are never transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your score and ranking on the monthly global leaderboard.
  • To save your quiz progress and preferences on your device.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore) — used for anonymous authentication and leaderboard data. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Leaderboard scores are reset at the beginning of each month. Anonymous user IDs and associated scores may be retained on Firebase servers until manually deleted. Local device data is removed when you uninstall the App or reset your statistics within the App.

5. Children's Privacy

Geology Daily is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

Since we only store an anonymous ID and a display name of your choice, there is no personally identifiable information to access or delete. If you wish to remove your leaderboard entry, you can reset your statistics from the App settings, which will also remove your Firebase data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
